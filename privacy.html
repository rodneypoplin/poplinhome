Information collected

The only data processed by this system is:

  • One cell phone number (the operator's own).
  • Message content generated by systems the operator owns (server alerts, home-automation events, appointment reminders).
  • Timestamps of message delivery, for the operator's own auditing and reliability checks.
  • Twilio delivery metadata (message SID, status codes) retained by Twilio per their standard retention policy — not accessed by any third party.

How information is used

Solely to deliver operational alerts from the operator's systems to the operator's own cell phone. No other purpose. No profiling. No advertising. No resale. No sharing.

Sharing

No personal data is shared with:

  • Advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing affiliates
  • Any third party, ever

Messages are transmitted via Twilio, which acts as the carrier-messaging service provider. Twilio's privacy practices are governed by their own policy at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Retention

The operator retains message logs on their own servers for approximately 30 days for debugging and reliability analysis, after which they are deleted or automatically rotated out. Twilio retains delivery metadata per their own policy.

Your rights

Because this system has one user (the operator), standard data-subject requests apply only to that operator. If you believe a message was sent to you in error, contact the operator at the address on the Contact page.
