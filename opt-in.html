How consent was captured

This is a single-user, self-authorized system. The operator configured the sending number in their own Twilio account, provisioned the receiving number as their personal cell, and authorized messaging to that cell by enabling it in their own account settings. No third parties are on the distribution list.

Revoking consent

The operator may revoke consent at any time by:

  • Texting STOP to the sending number (immediate effect, standard carrier unsubscribe)
  • Disabling the sending number at the source in the operator's Twilio account
  • Removing or suspending the Twilio project
